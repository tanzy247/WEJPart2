Beach

In the evening we headed to the beach to see the largest colony of little blue penguins returning to their nests.

Within a 1 hour period, 756 penguins were counted coming ashore! Shockingly, the most on record is over 5000! I also couldn’t believe how loud the calls of the chicks were from the nests as their parents returned.
