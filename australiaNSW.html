Dorrigo National Park

This is a World Heritage listed rainforest.

Nearby we visited a koala hospital where they care for injured koalas from the wild. Here they rehabilitate them before releasing them back into the wild. Unfortunately, sometimes they injuries are too severe and they would not survive in the wild, so instead they are looked after at the hospital.
