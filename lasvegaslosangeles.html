Day 1

After a last few chilled days in Mexico, we headed to Las Vegas to surprise Juliebear + Yorkie.

We visited some of the incredible flower Christmas displays inside some of our favourite hotels. As well as the flamingos at the flamingo hotel. We then enjoyed a few drinks and an all you can eat buffet, which was just incredible. (Especially after travelling on a budget for 3 months, we could barley move after.)

Day 2

We hired a car and travelled outside of Vegas.

First we visited Boulder City, which is where the workers and families lived who built Hoover Dam. We visited a museum which had an amazing informative video. We were so impressed with the engineering - particularly at the time with the resources in the middle of the dessert - but also because of the impact the dam had on the whole of America, preventing floods and improving farming even down to Mexico.

We then obviously visited the Hoover Dam itself.
