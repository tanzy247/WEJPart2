Day 1

We flew from Sydney to Uluru.

In the afternoon we visited Kata Tjuta - a collection of large domed rock formations. Here we hiked around the rocks and our guide explained how Kata Tjuta and Ayres Rock were formed.

In the evening we headed to Ayers Rock for sunset before sleeping under the stars.

Day 2

An early start we visited Uluru for sunrise and spent the morning walking around the rock.

The circumference of the rock is around 10km! Our guide took us to some of the caves inside the rock, where he explained how the aboriginal people used to live and hunt within the area.

Lots of the cave paintings tell stories from the aboriginal people.

In the afternoon we headed into the bush, where we had another night sleeping under the stars. We were able to spot the Southern Cross, the North Star, The 7 Sisters and Scorpio.

Day 3

Another early start as we woke for sunrise at the Kings Canyon. Here we did a 3 hour Hike on the rim of the Canyon.

In the afternoon we headed back to Ayres Rock, where we watched the sunset over Uluru and Kata Tjuda.

Day 4

We woke for our final sunrise, which was absolutely stunning. Watching the stars disappear as the sun came up. We then visited a camel farm where we were able to feed and stroke the camels.

The singing in Spanish was not a success.
